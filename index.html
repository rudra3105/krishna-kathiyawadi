About Krishna Kathiyawadi

At Krishna Kathiyawadi, we serve the heart of Gujarat on every plate. With authentic recipes passed down through generations, our kitchen blends tradition and taste to offer you a truly homely dining experience.

From spicy Ringan no Olo and Sev Tameta to wholesome Bajri Rotlo and cooling Masala Chaas — every dish is made with love, purity, and a pinch of village warmth. Whether you're here for lunch with family or a quick thali on the go, we promise food that feels like home.
