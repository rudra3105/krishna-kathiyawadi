About Krishna Kathiyawadi

Krishna Kathiyawadi Hotel was founded in 2005 by the Bhalodiya Brothers in Makarpura, Vadodara, with a vision to bring authentic Kathiyawadi flavors to the community. The hotel started with a simple menu of Kathiyawadi thalis, priced at just ₹18, and quickly won the hearts of locals. Due to immense customer support and love, we expanded from a seating capacity of 24 to 80, with separate AC and non-AC sections to provide a more comfortable dining experience.

The hotel’s success led to the opening of our second branch at Uma Char Rasta, Waghodia, with a dedicated team including Prakash Banugariya, Sanjay Shekhda, Paril Bhalodiya, and Ashish Savsani. As the demand for a more varied menu grew, we opened our third, most famous branch in 2022, located opposite L&T Knowledge City on NH-48, Waghodia, Vadodara. Here, we expanded our offerings to include Kathiyawadi, Punjabi, and Chinese cuisines, providing something for every palate.

In 2022, we also ventured into franchising with our first franchise in Odhav, Ahmedabad, operated by Jayendrasinh, Navdipsinh, and Dhananjaysinh. Responding to the increasing demand for Gujarati snacks and café-style dining, we launched our fifth branch, Krishna Kathiyawadi Live Snacks, Cafe & Fine Dine, on NH-48 in Tarsali in 2024.

The success and expansion of Krishna Kathiyawadi Hotel would not have been possible without the hard work and dedication of the following main owners:
Bhalodiya BhagavanjiBhai GopalBhai,
Bhalodiya NarendraBhai GopalBhai (JagadishBhai),

Their leadership, passion, and commitment to quality have made Krishna Kathiyawadi Hotel a trusted name in the world of Gujarati cuisine. We are proud to continue our journey, serving delicious food and unforgettable dining experiences to our loyal customers.
